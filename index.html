    What is the Pomodoro Technique?

    The Pomodoro Technique involves breaking your work into 25-minute intervals (called "Pomodoros") followed by short breaks. This cycle helps maintain your focus and prevents mental fatigue, making it a favorite tool for students, professionals, and anyone aiming to work smarter.

    This app is inspired by the Pomodoro Technique, a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo to boost focus and productivity.

    • Improve Work-Life Balance: Encourages regular breaks, reducing burnout.
